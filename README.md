# ecommerce-sales-analysis
Analysis of 99k+ e-commerce orders using Python — revenue trends, regional performance and delivery insights.

## Overview
Analysis of 99,000+ real e-commerce orders to identify revenue trends, 
regional performance, and delivery efficiency using Python.

## Key Findings
- November 2017 was peak revenue month — driven by Black Friday demand spike
- São Paulo (SP) dominates both order volume and total revenue
- 91.9% of orders delivered early — suggesting conservative estimate strategy
- Santa Catarina (SC) shows worst delivery delays, likely due to distance 
  from main SP logistics hub

## Business Recommendations
1. Scale logistics capacity in October-November for Black Friday
2. Investigate regional courier partnerships in southern states
3. Leverage early delivery rate as a marketing advantage
